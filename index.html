Our Love Story

They say opposites attract… and maybe they're right. We met at work: he was calm, easygoing and patient; she was stressed, overthinking everything and always running at full speed. Somehow, it worked from the very beginning. While she complicates things, he simplifies them. While she overthinks, he reminds her to breathe. And somewhere in those differences, we found the perfect balance. Almost 7 years later, we still choose each other every single day — through conversations, laughter, plans, little adventures and many sunsets together. We learned that love can also be found in calm moments, quiet company and the feeling of being exactly where you belong. And now, here we are, ready for the next chapter. 🤍
